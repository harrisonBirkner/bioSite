Hobbies

One of Jaryn's many hobbies is coffee. She currently works as a barista, and enjoys perfecting her latte art while on and off the job. Latte art is, like the name suggests, an art. It is a complex process that involves steaming milk and pouring it on top of espresso in such a way that ornate and simple patterns, such as hearts, tulips, or even swans, on top of the coffee. I found a very nice explanation here at livescience.com that goes into great detail on the science of the process.

Music is a huge part of Jaryn's life. She loves a wide array of artists across genres, including R&B, 60's, 70's, and various current artists as well. She especially loves musicals. She has seen local productions, as well as in Des Moines, or even off-broadway in Chicago. If a musical exists, Jaryn will know about it. In addition, she's been to various concerts and festivals, such as Hippocampus and Hinterland 2021.

One of Jaryn's greatest passions is the theatre, and has been in many productions since she was a child. Some of her favorite she has been in include Alice in Wonderland, Beauty and the Beast, and, most recently, Pippin. She especially loves musicals, where she gets to exercise her great singing and musical talent.
